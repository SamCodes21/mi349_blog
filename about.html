About Sam Clara

Hello! I’m Sam, the author behind this nature blog. I’ve always been fascinated by the natural world, especially the tiny details we often overlook. Whether it's watching birds in the backyard or hiking through bug-filled forests, I'm constantly learning from nature.

I started this blog to share my curiosity and knowledge with others who find wonder in the outdoors. There's always something new to discover, and I'm excited to keep writing about the little (and big) things that make our world amazing.

Fun Facts About Me:

  • I once raised a caterpillar until it became a butterfly.
  • My favorite animal is the octopus.
  • I’ve visited 12 national parks and counting!
